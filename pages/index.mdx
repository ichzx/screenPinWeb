# ScreenPin

import Image from 'next/image'

ScreenPin is an innovative iOS app that displays your memo information as Live Activity on your lock screen, providing you with a convenient way to view and manage important information.

Now you can access and track your memos anytime, anywhere directly on your lock screen, without unlocking your phone. The lock screen becomes your personal reminder center, helping you stay efficient and organized in your busy daily life.
