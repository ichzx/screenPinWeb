# Privacy

At ScreenPin, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. 
This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect any information collected or processed by our application.

1. Information Collection: ScreenPin does not collect any personal information or data from its users. 
The only information that is collected is the information you provide to us when you contact us for support or feedback. 
This information is used only to provide you with support or to improve the app.
2. User Data: ScreenPin does not store or transmit any user data. 
All memos and content created within the app are stored locally on the user's device and are not accessible or shared with any third parties.
3. Advertising and Third-Party Services: ScreenPin does not display any third-party advertisements and does not integrate with any third-party services that may collect user data.
4. Changes to the Privacy Policy: We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy, and we encourage users to review it periodically.
